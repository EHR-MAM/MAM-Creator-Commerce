🇬🇭 What Is Yes MAM?

Yes MAM is Africa's first creator-commerce infrastructure platform — a fully operational system that lets TikTok and social media influencers earn real income by selling products through a branded online store, without holding inventory, handling shipping, or having any technical skills.

Vendors list their products for free. Creators promote those products to their audiences. When a customer orders and the order is delivered, the creator earns 18% commission automatically, the vendor receives their payout, and MAM operates the infrastructure that makes it all run.
